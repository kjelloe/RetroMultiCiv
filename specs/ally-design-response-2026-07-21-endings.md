# Designer-ally response — endings, disasters, difficulty (VERBATIM-CONDENSED, 2026-07-21)

Answers to specs/ally-ask-2026-07-21b.md. Design language: dignified,
factual, era-aware. Routing: endgame-moments (helper item), engine
couplings into barb-sea-raids + disasters + difficulty slices (mailed
BEFORE build), advisor copy confirmed ready-when-table-lands.

## 1. Victory/defeat — "the Final Record" (Era Frame as canvas)

- SPACE ("Aspiration" ending): assembled ship in orbit FIRST (wireframe/
  blueprint glyph) → countdown 3-2-1 in console log → Launch fanfare →
  15-YEAR TIME-SKIP screen ("The voyage of the [Ship Name] continues…"
  over a starfield) → arrival text card ("Arrival at Alpha Centauri.
  Year: [Year]. A second home for humanity has been founded.") → final
  unique STELLAR ERA FRAME around the global history graph.
- CONQUEST: "Total Map" reveal — fog pulls back across the whole globe
  instantly. "The world is at peace. The colors of [Civ] span the
  horizon." No aggressive WINNER text — the quiet gravity of unification.
- RETIREMENT/SCORE: the Historian's Ending — "Chronicle of the World"
  summary; "Historians will remember [Leader] as a [score-derived
  title]." A closing book.
- DEFEAT: NEVER "Game Over" → **"The Fall of [Civilization]"** — capital
  glyph one last time, desaturation to grayscale, final log: "Your people
  will remember you for [best metric], but the story of the world
  continues without you." Replay button = witnessing history as a ghost.

## 2. Disasters + raids — "fair-but-grave", lesson-first

- DISASTER CARDS: dedicated Crisis frame (sharper edges, high-contrast).
  Headline `[TYPE] in [CITY]` + procedural disaster glyph + the
  RETROSPECTIVE line naming the prevention ("An Aqueduct would have
  mitigated this Plague." / "Temples calm the unrest; the people of
  [City] felt forgotten."). Tone: a leader receiving a report, not a
  victim of dice. ENGINE COUPLING: events must carry the preventedBy
  building id.
- RAIDS: ONE-TURN WARNING — any city (or nearby unit) with sea view logs
  "Foreign sails spotted off the coast of [City]" the turn BEFORE
  landing → a one-turn panic window (move a unit / change production).
  On landing: "Hostile Presence" notification + a unique low rhythmic
  drum cue. ENGINE COUPLING: the pre-landing sighting event,
  visibility-gated.

## 3. Difficulty — the "Hybrid Legacy" ladder (7 steps)

Trainer (entry/no-fail) → Chieftain → Warlord → Prince (the fair
baseline) → King → Emperor (authentic core 2-6) → God-Emperor
(beyond-authentic extreme). Reasoning: instant skill calibration for 4X
players via the authentic core; the outer bounds signal where this engine
extends the genre. Setup screen updates to the 7-step ladder.

## Engineering checklist (ally's own)

Space: 15-year skip. Conquest: globe reveal. Disasters: preventedBy id in
the modal. Raids: sails-spotted warning. UI: 7-step ladder.
Advisor copy: READY when the trigger+pedia-id table lands ("History as a
