# REVISION (same day, after the branding-specialist consultation) — SUPERSEDES the Founders recommendation above

## Founders WITHDRAWN as public title
Direct Steam-space collision + poor search distinctiveness. Its thematic
value is REPURPOSED as product language: "Founding Age" (era label),
"Founder's Record" (the replay/history interface), "Founders' Games"
(multiplayer event format), "founding a city" in UI copy.

## Revised brief (specialist's formulation, ally-adopted)
Favor CIVILIZATIONAL ACTS AND PROCESSES over common civic nouns — a title
about the making/continuation/shaping/recording/inheritance of history.
Not Empire/Civ/World/Founders alone.

## The clearance-first five (ally's creative ranking)
1. **The Work of Ages** — "most balanced: historical, deliberate,
   constructive, expansive"
2. **The Long Founding**
3. **The Long Design**
4. **The Shape of History**
5. **The Living Record**
Tier-2 (tradeoffs): A Lasting World, The Further World, The Known World,
The Starward Road, Worldmakers. Tier-3 (product-language, not master
titles): First Light, Commonwealth, Civil Dawn, Worlds in the Making,
Making History, Founders, The Great Work.

## Title architecture (three layers)
Distinct title + genre/fantasy line ("Build a civilization from first
settlement to the stars") + the differentiated promise:
**"Every history can be replayed."** — the player-facing translation of
the deterministic engine; usable IMMEDIATELY, title-independent.

## Domain guidance revised
Clearance checks FIRST, then register the winner: [title].eu canonical
(where it reflects real posture — stated in site copy, not inferred),
.com defensive at €18 if clean, .no €4 provenance, redirects to one
canonical; skip .game; .games optional. NO pre-registration of candidate
domains before clearance.
