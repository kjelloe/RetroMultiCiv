# To our designer ally — three open design questions (the unasked ones)

(Shareable as-is. Housekeeping first: your naming/domain verdict is filed
and ruled post-v1 with the domain-registration option flagged to Kjell now;
the branding brief for your specialist is ready to forward. These three are
the design questions we have NOT yet put to you.)

## 1. The victory moment (the biggest unspecced emotional beat)

We have your celebration frame for tech discoveries and the wonder splash.
But the GAME'S OWN endings have no designed moment yet: the space launch
(and the colony's arrival ~15 years later), conquest victory, the score/
retirement ending at 2100, and — the one players feel most — DEFEAT.
Today they get a functional endscreen (scores, history graph, replay
button). What should the final beat of a 545-turn game feel like, per
ending? Same never-auto-close rule presumably; the endscreen data is all
available (full history, per-civ timelines). This is the last "moment"
in the game without your fingerprints.

## 2. Disasters and raids — presenting authentic misfortune

Two authentic-1991 systems are entering the game: the eight classic
disasters (fire, flood, earthquake, famine, plague, piracy, volcano, civil
unrest — each preventable by a specific building) and barbarian SEA
LANDINGS (pirates beaching raiders on your coast). Design question: how
should bad luck LAND on screen so it reads as fair-but-grave rather than
random punishment? Our instinct: your envoy-modal gravity for the disaster
card ("FIRE ravages Thebes — a Aqueduct would have prevented this"), with
the PREVENTION always named — misfortune as a lesson, not a slap. And for
raids: how much warning does the coast get ("sails on the horizon"?) —
drama vs fairness. Your call on both tones.

## 3. Difficulty names — authentic or ours?

Our difficulty levels are named trainer → godemperor (original labels).
Civ 1's iconic ladder was Chieftain → Warlord → Prince → King → Emperor.
With authentic difficulty MODIFIERS now coming into the game, should the
NAMES go authentic too (provenance-clean, nostalgic), stay ours
(distinctive, avoids one more comparison surface), or hybrid (authentic
ladder + our trainer tier below and godemperor above)? Small thing, but
it sits on every setup screen.
