## Founder's Record — tone doctrine (ADOPTED, routes to helper)

**The unifying principle: the Chronicle exists regardless of
outcome** — every ending is an entry in the same record, not a
reaction to win/loss. Tone table:

| Ending | Tone | Compositional note |
|---|---|---|
| Space | earned, quiet, vast | launch = departure, not triumph |
| Conquest | grave, complete | world-brighten SLOW, a record not a fanfare |
| Score | reflective, archival | closing a book, not a scoreboard |
| Defeat | dignified, final | no music sting; the Chronicle still exists |

**DEFEAT capital glyph (ruled)**: use the fallen capital's OWN
procedural glyph, desaturated/fractured — recognition is the
emotional weight. Fallback if a damaged variant won't render
cleanly: the same glyph at reduced opacity, NO extra ruin
iconography ("absence reads as loss").
